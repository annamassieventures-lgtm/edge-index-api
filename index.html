The Methodology

THE INTELLIGENCE
METHODOLOGY.

Three analytical layers. One structured intelligence output.

01
Layer One

Behavioural Decision Architecture

Every individual has a distinct architecture for how they process pressure, authority, and opportunity. This is not personality — it is decision wiring. Under low-stakes conditions, most people operate within their natural decision profile. Under high stakes, behavioural patterns activate — often without awareness. The Edge Index maps this architecture precisely: where clarity lives, where distortion enters, and the specific conditions under which judgment becomes unreliable. These patterns are consistent. They can be identified. And once identified, they can be managed.

02
Layer Two

Timing Cycle Analysis

Decision environments are not static. They move through four identifiable phases across a 12-month cycle: expansion, contraction, volatility, and opportunity alignment. Each phase creates a different quality of decision environment. Expansion phases support initiation, risk-taking, and capital deployment. Contraction phases reward patience, reduction, and consolidation. Volatility phases amplify emotional pressure and distort judgment. Opportunity alignment phases are rare — they represent the highest-probability windows for consequential decisions. The Edge Index maps these phases individually, calibrated to each person's timing signature rather than market averages.
