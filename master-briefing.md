## WHAT THE EDGE INDEX IS

The Edge Index is a personalised decision-timing intelligence platform built on Human Design and astrology. Using birth data (date, time, location), the system generates a 17-section annual intelligence brief that maps an individual's cognitive timing windows across the year:

- When their judgment is clearest
- When risk perception is highest
- When to act aggressively and when to hold
- When to sign, negotiate, launch, walk away

**It is not predictive astrology. It is personalised chronobiology** — the same way athletes track HRV and sleep to know when to push and when to recover, The Edge Index maps the same cycle applied to financial, business, and life decisions.

**The core insight:** People optimise everything around their decisions — the data, the research, the advice — but they have never mapped *themselves* against the calendar. The Edge Index fills that gap.

## BRAND ARCHITECTURE

### The Edge Index (live)
Adult-focused, proactive intelligence. The *edge* over others. Trading, investing, AI, career, business, wellness — all adult high-stakes decisions. Dark, gold, sophisticated aesthetic. Telegram/app delivery. Not therapeutic — strategic. You're not processing, you're positioning.

### The Index (to build)
The white-label licensed platform. Any coaching brand, wellness company, or business can license The Index and brand it their own. The underlying engine is identical — HD + astrology + Claude AI. The surface is fully customisable. This is the $500M–$1B infrastructure play.

**Product architecture:** One birth chart input → multiple domain reports generated by different Claude prompts:
- Wealth & Finances domain
- Career & Purpose domain
- Health & Body domain
- Relationships domain
- Business & Decisions domain
- Creativity & Expression domain

**Delivery:** Daily/weekly/monthly timing updates. The annual report is the map. The subscription is the GPS — real-time navigation using the same map, updated continuously.

**The 6-month Q&A update:** Every 6 months the user answers a context questionnaire — relationship status, career phase, what they're building, what they're healing. The AI recalibrates the intelligence around their current life phase. Same chart, different lens. This makes it a lifetime product, not a one-time report.

### Mira (90% built)
Children, emotions, family dynamics. Licensed to child-focused businesses. WhatsApp delivery. Reactive — like a therapist in your pocket. Supportive, soft, nurturing tone. Tracks developmental phases from birth through adolescence. Same underlying HD/astrology engine, completely different output and tone. Needs: final connections, outreach system, launch.
