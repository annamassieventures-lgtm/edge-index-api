## NOTES FOR ANNA

- **Message 1 is a question, not a pitch.** This is critical. Questions get replies. Pitches get ignored.
- **"Other communities using it" in Message 2** — once you have your first yes (even a free beta), this is true. Don't say it until then; swap for "I've been running beta testing with a small group."
- **The retention angle** is what lands with admins. They think about churn constantly. Individual member results are secondary to them.
- **Founding beta price ($500)** is low enough to get a fast yes. The goal is signed clients, not maximum price per client right now.
- **Follow up on Telegram first** — admin response rates on Telegram DM are much higher than Discord.
