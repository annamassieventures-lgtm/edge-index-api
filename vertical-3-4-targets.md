- **Key selling point:** Reduces decision anxiety, increases conversion confidence, extends client lifecycle
- **Timeline:** Offer for founding beta partners at $500–$2k/month (3-month min)

- **Key selling point:** Shorter time-to-placement, deeper transformation, client retention (report = ongoing reference tool)
